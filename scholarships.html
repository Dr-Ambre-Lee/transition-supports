How to Apply

Start by downloading and completing the scholarship application above. Gather all required documents, including transcripts, letters of recommendation, and personal statements. Submit your completed application by the deadline.

Tips for Success

Start early and give yourself plenty of time to complete applications. Proofread carefully and ask someone to review your work. Be honest and authentic in your personal statements. Follow all instructions carefully.

Stay Organized

Keep track of deadlines using a calendar or spreadsheet. Create a checklist for each scholarship's requirements. Save copies of all submitted applications. Set reminders for upcoming deadlines.

Need Help?

Questions about the scholarship application? Need assistance completing the forms? Contact your Transition Coordinator for support and guidance throughout the application process.

Scholarship Opportunities

Finding and applying for scholarships is an important part of planning for your post-secondary education. Scholarships can help reduce the cost of college, vocational training, or other educational programs. We're here to support you through the application process.

Scholarship Application

Download our comprehensive scholarship application to get started on your journey to funding your education.

Download Application

How to Apply

Start by downloading and completing the scholarship application above. Gather all required documents, including transcripts, letters of recommendation, and personal statements. Submit your completed application by the deadline.

Tips for Success

Start early and give yourself plenty of time to complete applications. Proofread carefully and ask someone to review your work. Be honest and authentic in your personal statements. Follow all instructions carefully.

Additional Resources

Looking for more scholarship opportunities? Check out Fastweb, Scholarships.com, and Federal Student Aid for thousands of scholarships.

Need Help?

Questions about the scholarship application? Need assistance completing the forms? Contact your Transition Coordinator for support and guidance throughout the application process.
